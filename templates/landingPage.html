Phishing Awareness Tips
Stay Safe Online

Phishing is a cyber attack that uses disguised email as a weapon. The goal is to trick the email recipient into believing that the message is something they want or need — for instance, a request from their bank or a note from someone in their company — and to click a link or download an attachment.

  • Always verify the sender's email address.
  • Never click on unknown links or download unverified attachments.
  • Look out for spelling and grammar mistakes, which are common in phishing emails.
  • Contact your IT department if you suspect a phishing attempt.
